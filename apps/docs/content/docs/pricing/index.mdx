## Self-hosting

You can self-host Upstreet Agents **for free** for personal use.

For commercial uses, contact us at [support@upstreet.ai](mailto:support@upstreet.ai).

## API Usage Costing

This document outlines the costs associated with various models and services available through our API.
All costs are in USD.

$1 USD is equivalent to 1000 Upstreet credits.
